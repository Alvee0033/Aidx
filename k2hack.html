About the competition

Build with the world’s fastest reasoning model.

We are launching a two-stage global hackathon powered by K2 Think. Join the K2 Think Hackathon at MBZUAI.

Stage 1

Global Idea Call — submit a one-page proposal by October 26, outlining how K2 Think can power new applications, real-world use cases, or even entirely new user experiences.

Stage 2

Abu Dhabi 48h Build Challenge — the top 10 teams will be invited to Abu Dhabi for an intensive 48-hour hackathon to turn their ideas into working demos, supported by mentors and resources.

Competition guidelines

Important information

How it works

Stage 1 - Global idea call

  • Submit a one-page PDF: problem, how you’ll use K2 Think, impact, envisioned demo
  • Selection criteria: leverage of K2 Think, clarity, innovation, impact potential, feasibility
  • Support: Office hours and technical workshops during the application window

Stage 2 - Abu Dhabi 48-hour build

  • Top 10 teams fly to MBZUAI in Abu Dhabi; travel and meals covered
  • Deliverables: working demo, 3-minute pitch deck, brief technical notes
  • Judging criteria: depth of K2 Think integration, working prototype, user value and impact, creativity and innovation, technical execution, and pitch clarity
Who can apply?

Builders we’re looking for

  • Students eager to push cutting-edge AI into the real world
  • Researchers needing advanced math reasoning
  • Developers building for millions of users
  • Founders exploring ventures powered by ultra-fast reasoning

Why K2 Think

  • ~2,000 tokens/sec enables entirely new product experiences
  • Superior math reasoning for complex problems

Everything you need to know

Frequently asked questions

Who can apply?
Anyone worldwide — students, researchers, developers, or founders.
How many people per team?
Up to 4 members, with at least one developer and one PM/UX role.
Do I need to build on K2 Think?
Yes.
What do I submit in Stage 1?
A one-page idea proposal (problem, solution, K2 Think role, demo vision). Review “What your proposal must include” under Competition guidelines section above.
What happens in Stage 2?
Top 10 teams will fly to Abu Dhabi for a 48-hour build and live demo.
What costs are covered?
Travel grant that covers flight, accommodation, and meals.
What can winners get?
Your feature goes live in K2 Think, travel grants all covered, early access to K2 Think future releases and fast-track hiring opportunities.
What’s the judging criteria?
K2 Think integration, working demo, innovation, impact, technical execution, and pitch clarity.
Do I need to be physically in Abu Dhabi?
Yes, for Stage 2. The hackathon is in-person only.
Do I need prior experience with K2Think?
No — we’ll provide docs, workshops, and technical support to help you get started.
Will there be mentorship?
Yes — mentors from K2 Think team, G42, and the wider AI ecosystem will support teams during the hackathon.
Will you provide API access?
We won’t provide API yet for Stage 1 to ensure all shortlisted teams start at the same baseline for the 48 Build.
However, if you’d like to try early, the model’s open on Hugging Face, so you can deploy it yourself; you can also test via the K2 web app.
For Stage 2, shortlisted teams will get full API access to build their demos.
